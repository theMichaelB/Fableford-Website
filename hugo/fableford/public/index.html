What is Fableford?

Fableford is a publishing platform where writers create stories that connect through character interactions in a shared world.

Unlike role-playing games or character development tools, Fableford is about creating and publishing complete stories that readers can enjoy. Each writer owns their characters and contributes to a growing universe of interconnected narratives.

Writers earn money when readers engage with their stories, creating a sustainable ecosystem for creative work.

Fableford is...

A publishing platform for writers to create interconnected stories
A shared universe where characters from different writers can interact
A way for writers to earn money from their creative work

Fableford is not...

A role-playing game or forum
Just a character creation tool
A traditional social media platform

How Fableford Works

Writers Create Characters & Stories

Each writer develops their own character and publishes stories about their character's life in Fableford.

Characters Interact & Connect

Writers collaborate to create interactions between their characters, building relationships that span multiple stories.

A Network of Stories Emerges

Over time, a rich tapestry of interconnected narratives develops, creating a living world with depth and history.

Readers Follow Characters & Connections

Readers can follow individual characters or explore relationships, discovering the world through different perspectives.

Stories Connect in Fableford

Example: How Stories Connect
Eleanor's Story

Eleanor, who runs the bookshop, writes about hosting a book club meeting for local literature enthusiasts.

Written by Writer #1
Sophie's Story

Sophie, who teaches at the local school, writes about attending Eleanor's book club and discussing her favorite novel.

Written by Writer #2
Daniel's Story

Daniel, who's renovating the old vicarage, writes about stopping by Eleanor's bookshop to find local history books.

Written by Writer #3

These three separate stories, by different writers, now form a connected narrative thread that readers can follow from multiple perspectives.

Over time, hundreds of such connections create a rich, interconnected world!
